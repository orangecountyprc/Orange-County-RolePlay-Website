Florida Highway Patrol (FHP)

The Florida Highway Patrol is responsible for enforcing traffic laws, conducting high‑visibility patrols, responding to major roadway incidents, and ensuring the safety of Florida’s state highways. Troopers specialize in traffic enforcement, DUI interdiction, crash investigations, and rapid emergency response.

Orlando Police Department (OPD)

OPD protects the City of Orlando through urban patrols, criminal investigations, tactical operations, and community policing. Officers respond to calls ranging from traffic stops to major crimes, ensuring safety in one of Florida’s busiest metropolitan areas.

Orange County Sheriff's Office (OCSO)

OCSO provides county‑wide law enforcement, including patrol, investigations, civil process, and specialized units such as K9, Marine Patrol, and Emergency Response. Deputies serve both urban and rural communities across Orange County.

Orange County Fire Rescue (OCFR)

OCFR responds to structure fires, medical emergencies, vehicle accidents, hazardous materials calls, and rescue operations. Firefighters are cross‑trained as EMTs or paramedics, providing life‑saving care across the county.

Florida Department of Transportation (FDOT)

FDOT manages roadway safety, traffic flow, incident response, and infrastructure operations. Members assist with lane closures, accident scenes, roadway hazards, and statewide transportation support.
