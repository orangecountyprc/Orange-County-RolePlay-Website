About OCRP

Orange County Roleplay is a Florida-based ERLC community built for realism, professionalism, and immersive emergency response gameplay.

Built for Realism

Florida-Based Roleplay

OCRP was created to deliver a truly immersive Florida emergency response experience. Every department, uniform, vehicle, and procedure is crafted to reflect real-world Orange County operations.

Professional Departments

Our departments follow structured training, realistic protocols, and professional standards. Whether you're patrolling highways, responding to fires, or managing traffic operations, OCRP delivers a polished and authentic experience.
